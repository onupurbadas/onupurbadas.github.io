**Consultant, Institute for Social Science Research, University of Massachusetts Amherst (September 2023 - Present)**

I offer consultation to graduate students across disciplines on research methods and econometric tools. I assist them with the analysis techniques including but not limited to panel/longitudinal data, multivariate regression, causal inference and other statistical methods using STATA. I have also conducted workshops focusing on using STATA for statistical analysis for students, faculty and staff with a beginner and intermediate understanding of the software tool. Consulting graduate students across mutliple disciplines with their research has encriched my ability to work collaboratively on solving research questions on social issues.
